Connect Your Canvas Account

To display your assignments, TuskCS needs a Canvas API token. Follow these steps to generate one:

  1. Go to canvas.fullerton.edu and log in.
  2. Click your Account icon in the top-left corner, then select Settings.
  3. Scroll down to the Approved Integrations section.
  4. Click + New Access Token.
  5. Enter a purpose (e.g., TuskCS) and click Generate Token.
  6. Copy the token shown — it will only be displayed once.
  7. Paste it into the field below and click Save Token.
